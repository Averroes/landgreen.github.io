  • double slit experiment
  • wave-particle duality
  • de Broglie wavelength

    • At quantum scales all particles act like waves towards the particles they are not in contact with. This wave-particle duality is a central idea in quantum mechanics.

    The wave nature of particles is easy to see at the subatomic scale for things like electrons, protons, or photons. At larger scales the wave nature is less noticeable because of the difficulty isolating larger objects. This is why humans, or cats, never manifest quantum effects.
