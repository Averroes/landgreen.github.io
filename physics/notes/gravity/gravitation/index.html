Universal Gravitation

Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation

All particles are attracted to each other, but the attraction is divided by the square of the distance between the particles.

$$ F = \frac{GM_{1}M_{2}}{r^{2}} $$

F = force of gravity [N, Newtons, kg m/s²] vector
G = universal gravitation constant [N(m/kg)²] scalar
M = mass [kg, kilograms] scalar
r = distance between the center of each mass[m, meters] scalar
